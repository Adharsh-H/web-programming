TOURIST PLACES IN PALAKKAD
NELLIYAMPATHY HILLS

From the town of Nenmara in Palakkad district, the cloud-caressed peaks of the majestic Nelliyampathy Hill ranges are a sight to behold. The height of the hills ranges from approximately 467 m to 1572 m and it has an extremely calming effect on all who view it. To reach Nelliyampathy, one has to take the road starting from Nenmara that proceeds to the Pothundy Dam. There are about 10 hairpin bends that have to be negotiated on the way to Nelliyampathy.Top sites in Nelliyampathy include:
  1. Pothundy Dam
  2. Seethargundu View Point
  1. Seethargundu WaterFalls
  2. Nelliyampathy Forest Reserve
MALAMPUZHA dAM

Malampuzha Dam is the largest dam and reservoir in Kerala, located near Palakkad, in the state of Kerala in South India, built post independence by the then Madras state. Situated in the scenic hills of the Western Ghats in the background it is a combination of a masonry dam with a length of 1,849 metres and an earthen dam with a length of 220 metres making it the longest dam in the state .Tourist attractions to see in Malampuzha:
  • Rock Garden
  • Snake Park
  • Thread Garden
  • Fantasy Park
